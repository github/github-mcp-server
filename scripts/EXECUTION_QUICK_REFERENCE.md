## DAILY WORKFLOW

### 3 Days Before Ex-Date
1. Identify upcoming ex-date from dividend calendar
2. Calculate shares to purchase: `(Available Cash √ó 0.33) / Current Price`
3. Set buy order for 2 days before ex-date

### On Buy Day
- Execute buy order at market open
- Confirm shares received
- Set calendar reminder for sell date

### Day After Ex-Date (Sell Day)
- Execute sell order at market open (target: +0.5% to +1.0%)
- Confirm proceeds
- Wait 2-3 days for dividend to settle
- Recycle capital to next opportunity

### Calculate Profit
```
Profit = (Sell Price - Buy Price) √ó Shares + Dividend Income
Example: ($43.10 - $42.50) √ó 200 + $64 = $184 per $8,500 deployed = 2.2%
```

## QUICK TROUBLESHOOTING

**Problem: Dividend amount lower than expected**
- Solution: Adjust future position sizes down, monitor for cuts

**Problem: Price down on ex-date**
- Solution: Exit at predetermined stop-loss (-1% to -2%)

**Problem: Can't fill buy order**
- Solution: Use limit order 0.25% below ask, or skip to next cycle

**Problem: Dividend cut announced**
- Solution: Reduce position size 20-30% or remove temporarily

**Problem: Running low on cash**
- Solution: Wait for current positions to close before new buys
