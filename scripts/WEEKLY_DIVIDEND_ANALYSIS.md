## RISK FACTORS WITH MORE MONTHLY ETFS

### 1. **Execution Fatigue**
- 6-10 monthly ETFs = trading almost daily
- High chance of missing buy/sell windows
- Solution: Use automated alerts and pre-set orders

### 2. **Covered Call Risk**
- QYLD, RYLD, DJIA, XYLD all use covered calls
- These **underperform in bull markets** (capped upside)
- If S&P rallies 20%, these might only gain 5-8%
- Solution: Accept lower price appreciation for income

### 3. **Correlation Risk**
- All 6 monthly ETFs use similar strategies
- If covered calls fall out of favor, all suffer
- Solution: Keep quarterly ETFs for diversification

### 4. **Tax Complexity**
- 92+ trades = extensive recordkeeping
- More short-term gains = higher taxes
- Solution: Use tax software, keep meticulous records

### 5. **Dividend Cut Risk**
- Covered call dividends vary with volatility
- If VIX drops, dividends decrease
- Solution: Monitor option premiums, adjust expectations
